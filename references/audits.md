## 16 — SEARCH INTENT MAP

```
For this business type + service areas:
Map keyword universe into 4 buyer stages:
Stage 1 UNAWARE: has a problem, doesn't know what it's called
e.g. "water coming through ceiling", "AC making loud noise"
Stage 2 AWARE: knows the problem, researching solutions
e.g. "how to fix leaking roof", "why is AC not cooling"
Stage 3 COMPARING: evaluating options and providers
e.g. "plumber vs DIY", "how to choose HVAC company"
Stage 4 READY: wants to book NOW
e.g. "emergency plumber [city]", "HVAC repair near me"
For each stage:
  ∙ 10 example keywords with volume tier
  ∙ conversion rate comparison (Stage 4 converts 5-10x Stage 2)
  ∙ content/page type that should target these
Content strategy per stage:
Stage 4 → service pages + GBP (money)
Stage 3 → comparison + FAQ pages
Stage 2 → educational blog → funnels to service
Stage 1 → problem content → builds early trust
Top 5 Stage 4 keywords: exact 90-day ranking plan for each
```
