# PRESET — AI SEARCH READY

**Goal:** get cited in Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Bing Copilot
**Time:** 2-3 weeks
**Sequence:** Prompt 22 → 18 → 17 → 23 → 16

## Why this order

22 runs the GEO audit first — establishes your current AI search visibility and identifies exactly what signals are missing.

18 builds entity signals — your business needs to exist as a verified entity in Google's knowledge graph before AI systems will cite it consistently. This is infrastructure.

17 finds content gaps and writes briefs for content that's structured to be quoted by AI systems. Problem-awareness content is the highest-value format.

23 scores your existing content against EEAT standards — Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness. Generic content no longer gets cited.

16 maps the full keyword universe to buyer journey stages — so your content targets searches that convert, not just searches that get traffic.

## Note

This preset applies to every business type. AI search affects local businesses, SaaS, e-commerce, and content sites equally. The window to build these signals before your competitors is open now.
