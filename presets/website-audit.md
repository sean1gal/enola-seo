# PRESET — WEBSITE AUDIT

**Goal:** find and fix the pages costing you rankings right now
**Time:** one week
**Sequence:** Prompt 21 → 24 → 09 → 10 → 12 → 13

## Why this order

21 runs the technical scan first — a 6-second mobile load time cancels out every other fix. Know what's broken technically before you optimize anything.

24 clears hard blockers — if something is actively broken, fix it before you build on top of it.

09 finds the keyword gaps — searches competitors rank for that you don't. This tells you which pages to fix and which to build.

10 finds the page 2 opportunities — rankings you're already close to winning. A jump from position 15 to 5 is worth more than 10 new pages.

12 runs the Search Console sprint — turns your real ranking data into a 30-day fix plan with every piece of copy written.

13 aligns your messaging with what your customers actually say — turns competitor review language into homepage copy.
