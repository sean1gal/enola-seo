# PRESET — CONTENT RESCUE

**Goal:** recover rankings lost to decaying content
**Time:** 2-4 weeks
**Sequence:** Prompt 25 → 23 → 17 → 20

## Why this order

25 identifies what's decaying and why — content written 2-3 years ago loses ground to competitors who updated theirs last month. Find the highest-ROI recovery opportunities first.

23 scores your content against EEAT — pages losing rankings are almost always weak on Experience and Expertise signals. The rewrite brief for your lowest-scoring page is included.

17 fills gaps left by decaying content — new searches your competitors have captured while your pages were sliding.

20 measures what moved — run after 30 days to see which refreshes produced ranking recovery and which need another pass.

## Note

Do not start new content until your best existing pages are refreshed. Pages that already have authority are almost always faster and cheaper to recover than starting from scratch.
