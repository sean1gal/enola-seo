# PRESET — AUTHORITY SPRINT

**Goal:** build the backlinks and citations that make every other ranking signal stick
**Time:** one month
**Sequence:** Prompt 15 → 14 → 18 → 16

## Why this order

15 fixes inconsistent citations first — wrong NAP data across directories creates trust signal conflicts that suppress rankings. Fix the foundation before building on top of it.

14 builds links from real sources — 90-day plan with complete outreach emails written. 2-4 real contextual links per month compounds faster than 20 directory submissions.

18 builds entity signals — links land on a verified entity, not just a website. Google's understanding of your business as a real-world entity affects how much it trusts every signal.

16 maps the intent landscape — so authority building targets the searches that convert, not just the ones with the highest volume.
