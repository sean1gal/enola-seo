# PRESET — SAAS AND B2B START

**Goal:** keyword strategy, content moat, and AI search visibility for online businesses
**Time:** 3-4 weeks
**Sequence:** Prompt 21 → 09 → 16 → 22 → 18 → 17 → 14

## Why this order

21 fixes technical issues first — slow load times and crawlability problems undermine everything else.

09 finds keyword gaps with real buyer intent — searches your competitors rank for that you don't, filtered to commercial and transactional intent.

16 maps the full keyword universe by buyer journey stage — SaaS and B2B buyers research for weeks before contacting anyone. You need to be present across all four stages, not just Stage 4 (ready to buy).

22 and 18 build AI search and entity signals — B2B buyers increasingly ask AI assistants before they search. If you're not in those answers during the research window, you don't exist in their consideration set.

17 fills content gaps — the problem-awareness and solution-comparison content that captures buyers before they're ready to talk to sales.

14 builds the backlinks that make every ranking signal stick.
