## PHRASES TO SUGGEST (not dictate) IN YOUR ASK

When asking for a review, these prompts help customers write useful reviews without coaching them:

- "If you mention what we fixed and how fast we got there, that helps people searching for the same thing."
- "Even just 2-3 sentences about what happened and how it went is perfect."

Do not say: "Please mention that we offer same-day service." That's coaching and violates Google's guidelines. Instead, mention it naturally in conversation before asking.
